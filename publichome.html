Welcome To Agora

Agora is an invite-only chatroom website, founded and designed by John Cinek.

Created in November of 2023, Agora aims to cultivate and inspire deep conversations amongst its users, and provide a safe space for people from all walks of life to convene and socialize with one another. Whether you want to discuss the current socioeconomic and political climates of the world, or simply chat about the latest movies, music, or events in pop culture, Agora has a chatroom for everyone.

To learn more, be sure to visit this site's About page.

Want to become a member? Visit the Contact page to request an invite.
