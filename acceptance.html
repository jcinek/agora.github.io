Acceptance

As previously declared, the chatrooms on this website are invite‐only. That being said, no one will ever be denied entry based on their race, religion, sexuality, gender identity, or national or ethnic origin. Rather, this site is intended to serve as a safe space for people from all walks of life, where discriminatory and hateful comments and actions amongst site members will not be tolerated. Per this site's Terms of Service:

"You may not transmit any Content that is unlawful, offensive, upsetting, intended to disgust, threatening, libelous, defamatory, obscene or otherwise objectionable. Examples of such objectionable Content include, but are not limited to, the following...Defamatory, discriminatory, or mean-spirited content, including references or commentary about religion, race, sexual orientation, gender, national/ethnic origin, or other targeted groups."

To put this clearly: if you are someone who wants to engage in any from of discrimination or hate speech, this site is not for you. As it stands, one of the primary reasons this website is invite‐only is to ensure that an environment of tolerance, acceptance, and compassion is maintained. We're here to make friends, talk about our interests, and perhaps even dissect the problems of the world. In no way whatsoever does this site exist to instigate any additional problems. After all, we already have plenty of those, don't we?

If you have witnessed or been victim to any such behavior ‐ from any of our members ‐ please visit the Contact page and reach out right away.
